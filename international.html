INTERNATIONAL

We want you to feel as comfortable as possible when you transition into college so here is a list of events that you will be encountering during Freshman Orientation! Check out these events so you can feel prepared for your first week in college!

OFFICE FOR INTERNATIONAL STUDENTS AND SCHOLARS

This office is the best place to go to for help with anything! The staff here are trained to help find courses that can help you learn English, travel around St. Louis, with documentation for work and international travel, and so much more. Click on this title to be redirected to the office's website.

ENGLISH LANGUAGE PROGRAMS

The English Language Programs (ELP) provides classes and academic English language support designed to strengthen the English language proficiency of nonnative English speakers in the university community, and facilitate their academic and professional success at Washington University in St. Louis and beyond. Click on this title to be redirected to a website further explaining these programs.

PREPARE FOR THE USA

The best way to prepare for the US is to talk to current WashU students. There are many WashU student volunteers who would be more than happy to introduce students to the WashU lifestyle -- please contact the Undergraduate Admissions Office at (314) 935-6000 to ask for a student volunteer!
